The Cigna HealthcareSM PPO Network only provides access to care outside Kaiser Permanente states of CA, CO, GA, HI, MD, OR, VA, WA, and DC. The Cigna Healthcare PPO Network only provides access to care in non-Kaiser Permanente states and refers to providers (doctors, hospitals, specialists) contracted as part of the Cigna Healthcare PPO Network for Shared Administration. Benefits are not insured by Cigna or Affiliates.
