Care Outside of Kaiser

If you receive emergency care in a non-Plan hospital
1-800-777-7904 (TTY 711)

Call as soon as your condition is stabilized so that a Kaiser Permanente physician can discuss your medical information and coordinate your care with the treating physician.

For care options when traveling
1-951-268-3900 (TTY 711)

Visit kp.org/travel or call for urgent or emergency care available from any provider when traveling.
